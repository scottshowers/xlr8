## ğŸ’¬ FAQs

**Q: How much can I upload?**
A: Start with 5-10 files, grow to 50-100+ over time. No hard limit.

**Q: Can other team members see what I upload?**
A: Yes, Foundation Intelligence is shared across all users of the application.

**Q: Does it send data to external APIs?**
A: No! Everything stays local. Your Local LLM processes it on your server.

**Q: Can I delete files later?**
A: Yes, you can add and remove files anytime.

**Q: How long does indexing take?**
A: Usually seconds to minutes, depending on file size.

**Q: Will it slow down the application?**
A: No, indexing happens in background. No impact on performance.

**Q: What if I upload the wrong file?**
A: Just delete it from the list. No harm done.

**Q: Can I organize files into folders?**
A: Current version is flat structure. Folder organization planned for future.
