What We Found

Analysis detected 1,247 employee records (2.6% of workforce) with missing or invalid tax jurisdiction codes. These employees are concentrated in 3 states: California (512), Texas (423), and New York (312). Without valid locality codes, these employees may have incorrect tax withholdings.

Why It Matters

Missing tax jurisdictions can result in incorrect withholding calculations, exposing the organization to compliance penalties and employee dissatisfaction. California alone has aggressive local tax enforcement with penalties up to $50 per occurrence.
